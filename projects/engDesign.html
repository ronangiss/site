Introduction

  This report was completed as part of my engineering design class during the fall semester of my junior year at Tufts. Working in a team with two other students, we analyzed several components of a cold forging header machine. My main contributions in this report were the FEA analysis of the crankshaft, failure analysis of the crankshaft, and the crankshaft bearing analysis. Download the report here or read it below.
