Introduction

  This report was completed as part of my fluids class during the fall semester of my junior year at Tufts. In this simulation (ran in COMSOL Multiphysics) we examined the heat transfer and flow through a number of heat sinks with fins at different angles. To validate our simulations, we used experimental data from a research paper comparing straight and oblique fin heat sinks. Download the report here or read it below.
